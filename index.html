About Us

We are committed towards providing a platform for young innovators to develop ideas and implement them. Boasting numerous accolades from various national and international competitions, ARC continues to grow as one of the best student clubs in the robotics community of Nepal.
ARC has been organizing various training sessions and seminars on not just robotics, but in other disciplines as well. With events like workshops on AVR programming, PCB Designing, Training on Soldering, PythonBytes (a training on the basics of Python), and a joint collaboration titled ‘Together we Learn’, including Presentation Workshop, Web Designing and Teaching Machines to think (an introductory session on Machine Learning), ARC has time and again manifested as well as associated the enthusiastic minds of robotics with real field experience. Through these kinds of Trainings and Workshops, ARC has fostered many astute technical minds to supplement progress in their field of Robotics. In recent years, ARC has also developed a pamphlet distributing robot named “Qarisma” which applies face recognition technology. The purpose of this robot is to distribute pamphlets to any visitors passing by and greet them with a sweet gesture. This Robot was even featured in the top Social Media pages of Nepal.

Technorion Nepal 2023

Techfest, organized by IIT Bombay, is a premier global science and technology institute. It's grown from a small event in 1998 to Asia's largest festival, with 1,75,000 students from 2500+ Indian colleges, 500+ international colleges, and 7,000+ faculty and corporate participants. Since 2004, Techfest has gone international, attracting entries from various countries worldwide. In its 26th edition in 2023, Techfest will be held at IIT Bombay, India, and includes International Qualifiers in Nepal. Techfest Technorion is an expansion of Techfest, with zonal competitions held in multiple cities, including Nepal. It serves as a qualifier for the finale at IIT Bombay. Organized by Advanced Robotics Club (ARC), Technorion Nepal is the only route for Nepali students to participate in select Techfest events and qualify for the IIT Bombay finale. The first edition of Technorion in Nepal took place in 2019, and the upcoming event on November 12th, 2023, features competitions like Cozmo Clench, Meshmerize, CoDecode, and Techfest Olympiad. Winners of these competitions will be eligible to participate in the Techfest finale scheduled from December 16th to 18th at IIT Bombay.
